Privacy Policy

Sadhana ("the App") is a Hindu spiritual practice app developed as a personal devotional companion. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Spiritual practice data — Japa session counts, reading progress, streaks, and milestones
  • Personal reflections — Daily journal entries you create within the App
  • Preferences — Tab order, selected mantras, Panchang location, ambient sound settings, and other customization choices
  • Bookmarks and reading plans — Your saved positions and active reading plans

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not require account creation or sign-in
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools
  • We do not store any data on remote servers

Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions, each used solely for the stated purpose:

PermissionPurpose
MicrophoneAarti sing-along feature — listens to your singing to highlight the current lyric line. Audio is processed on-device and is never recorded or transmitted.
Speech RecognitionRecognizes aarti lyrics being sung to follow along with the text. Processing occurs on-device via Apple's Speech framework.
Photo Library (Save)Save verse cards as images to your photo library for sharing. The App does not read or access your existing photos.

You may deny any of these permissions, and the App will continue to function — only the specific feature requiring that permission will be unavailable.

Network Usage

The App may connect to the internet solely to download optional scripture content packs and fetch the Chalisa catalog. No personal data is transmitted. The App functions fully offline for all bundled content.

Data Security

All data is stored locally using Apple's standard frameworks (SwiftData). It is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. We have no access to your data.

Data Retention & Deletion

You control your data entirely. Deleting the App removes all associated data from your device. There is no cloud backup unless you have enabled iCloud device backups through your own Apple ID settings.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from any user, making it safe for individuals of all ages.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not share data with any third party.

Crash Diagnostics (MetricKit)

The App uses Apple's built-in MetricKit framework to receive crash and hang diagnostics for stability improvements. This data is processed locally on your device. When you have "Share with App Developers" or "Share iPhone Analytics" enabled in iOS Settings, similar diagnostic data may be shared with Apple and made available to developers through Xcode Organizer. We do not send this data to our own servers.
